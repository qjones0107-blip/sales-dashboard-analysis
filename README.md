# 📊 Sales Dashboard Analysis

This project analyzes sales data to uncover revenue trends, regional performance, and top-selling products. The goal is to generate insights that support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze sales performance across regions and products  
- Identify revenue trends over time  
- Highlight key drivers of business performance  

---

## 🛠 Tools Used 
- Python (data processing)  
- SQL (analysis concepts)  

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Order ID  
- Date  
- Region  
- Product  
- Category  
- Price  
- Quantity  
- Revenue

## 🔍 Key Insights
- East and North regions generate the highest revenue  
- Laptops and phones are the top-performing products  
- Electronics account for the majority of sales  
- Revenue remains consistent with slight growth trends  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions  
- Increase inventory for top-selling products  
- Bundle accessories with electronics to increase revenue
